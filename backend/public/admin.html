PC Parameters — Live Tuning Reference
Every active PC KPI with its current production tuning values (delay, hold, decay, weight). Use this to eyeball the algorithm inputs that drive your scenarios. Click any row to jump to the KPI Designer for that KPI.

Algorithm Reference

initialPC = avg_price × comm_rate × (weight/100)
DELAY → $0 | HOLD → full | DECAY → linear | EXPIRED → $0

TTC = delay + hold days.

PC Weight = fraction of a deal.

Decay is linear to $0.
